BANDARAGAMA

Weda Pansala(Purana Maha Viharaya)



This temple is well known for really talented
monks who can do celon western medical treatments.
Thats why this temple is called "Weda Pansala".
"Weda" means medical and "Pansala" means temple.
Its real name is "Puraana Maha Viharaya".
